Features

Meaningful Sharing

Clipline excels in sharing and embedding recorded video game sessions, tutorials, product reviews, talk shows, speeches, lectures, and sports coverage. Explore more samples.

Interactive Embeds

Want to enhance the Clipline experience on your blog or Twitter feed? Simply embed our Content Aware Video Player. It's responsive and designed to work across various modern browsers and devices. Start by tagging a video or choose from pre-tagged ones, then paste the embed code into your website.

Smart Tagging

Discover an intriguing moment in a video? On Clipline, tagging isn't restricted to video owners; any community member can contribute. Furthermore, our intelligent algorithms can suggest tags (currently in private beta) for a diverse range of content.

Benefit

Personalized Experience

ClipMine allows your viewers to explore video content and see specific parts of the videos they like.

Improve Engagement

Our customers have seen up to 50% higher engagement and lower early drop-off rates on their videos when embedded with ClipMine.

Actionable Analytics

Our in-video analytics (by invitation only) allow you to understand user engagement in terms of your content rather than timestamps. You'll exactly know which parts of the video are more likely to be clicked and how long users stay there.
